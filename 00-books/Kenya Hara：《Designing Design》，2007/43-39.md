![](images/b84cc15b04f22494682e829ffbbb16796025c4f7d4adaf5e1c1ed0d1a63a8b70.jpg) Mende has worked on several lighting projects for large-scale public spaces, including theSendai Mediatheque and Roppongi Hills. It's appropriate to say thatMende designs not simply lighting appliances, but light itself. That is, he is a designer of light as well as darkness. He became somewhat of a celebrity by organizing a team called The Lighting Detectives to conduct research on urban nighttime lighting. According to the team, vending machines emit the strongest light on city streets. The Detectives also report that convenience store lighting is abnormally intense. That intensity then probably makes a dominant impression on the nighttime streets of Tokyo. The Lighting Detectives team proves that taking sharp notice of these facts is a primary step towards urban design.
