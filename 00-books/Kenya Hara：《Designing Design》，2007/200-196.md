# Books as Information Sculpture RediscoveringBooks I do a lot of book design. And I like to compile things in book format. But the accelerated speed of the evolution of information technology has led to the foundation of more and more information formats. Under these circumstances, it may be time we consider the possibility that books have already stepped down from their traditional role as information medium. As far as speed, density and volume go, we can't even compare books to electronic media. Still, it's hard to come to the conclusion that the role of books has collapsed. Perhaps it's time we reevaluate what a book is. If we keep designing books in the conventional manner without going through this reevaluation, it would seem we're a bit out of step with the times. Let's take a long, cool look at paper as a material. As a medium, paper has assumed great responsibility.
