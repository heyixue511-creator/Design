What we should focus on is the fact that a product that is rationally targeted at a local market's level of desire will have advantageous influence in the global market. If marketing is conducted very precisely in an unsophisticated country, the products are also unsophisticated, and sell well in the local market. The opposite is also true, but causes no problems unless the products are circulated on a global scale. Now, if the sophisticated products flow into the market of an unsophisticated country, consumers are likely to be inspired, nurturing a desire to get products fromother sophisticated countries.
