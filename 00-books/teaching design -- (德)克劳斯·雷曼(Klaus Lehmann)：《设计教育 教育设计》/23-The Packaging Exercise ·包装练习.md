## The Packaging Exercise

A simple exercise to train inventiveness and understanding of material can be done by packaging a simple product with paper or cardboard. If you understand the characteristics of paper, you have already learned a lot. Packagíng products is a specialist job, which usually is done by specialised designers, but isopen for everybody who has goodideas and knows how to handle the material. Packages have become a prominent selling point. The package must first of all fulfil its function to protect and support a product. But in addition it conveys a meaning. It can tell the consumer what is inside, arouse his interest, display lifestyle, convey intelligence or wit, simplicity, tradition, abundance, or ecological consideration. A package is thegarment of a product and, just as human clothing, aims to clad the product and support its personality.

Design a package for ping pong balls with paper or cardboard using only a cutteras tool.

## Approaches:

First test the material, its pliability, its structure, its texture andcolour. Try packaging eggs, ping-pong balls, soap, cakes, or other simple things to start with. Check its practical functions; protection, handling, transport, stacking, or recycling. Check it for its meaning and expression, judge its formal and semantic quality and develop criteria for its evaluation.

## ·包装练习

通过使用纸张或纸板包装一种简单产品的练习，可以训练一个人的创新性，以及对材料特征的理解。如果你理解了纸张的特征，那么你已经知道很多。包装产品是一种非常特殊的工作，通常情况下是由经过特殊训练的设计师操作的，但实际上，这项工作也可以由富有绝佳创意的、了解如何处理材料的任何人来完成。包装已经成为一种众所周知的产品卖点之一。首先，包装必须起到最基本的保护和宣传产品的作用，而另外，它还蕴含着其他意义。它还可以告知消费者产品的信息，引起他们的兴趣，展示一种生活方式，传递智慧或才智、简约、传统、丰盈或环保讯息。包装是产品的服装，就像人类的服装一样，旨在包裹产品，并展示产品自身的个性。

使用纸张或纸板设计一种乒乓球的包装，工具仅可使用切割刀。

方法：

首先检验材料的柔韧性、结构、质地和颜色。可以先试试包装鸡蛋、乒乓球、肥皂、蛋糕或其他简单的东西。检查其实用效果如何：保护性、手提便携性、运输、储藏或回收等是否方便等。检查它是否传达了产品的信息和所要表达的含义，评判它的形式和语义质量，并总结出相关的评估标准。
