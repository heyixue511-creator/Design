Toyo Ito was in charge of the architectural design. ## Kaoru Mende and Matches The subject here is matches, which ignite when you strike them. Lately, it's unusual to find matches in the home. Even for cigarettes, we usually use lighters. There are fewer and fewer occasions in which we actually handle fire. A gas range gives off light, but electric cooking equipment is more and more popular. This being the case today, you might wonder why I chose matches as one of the subjects of the re-design project. Basically, the theme here is to design the fire that lies closest to home. Guessing that this probably falls within the realm of illumination professionals, I asked the lighting designer Kaoru Mende to work on matches. ![](images/6cf2753f12044c54a3842201b6bc836922ae213896ea641e8b45c70edaeb3087.jpg)
