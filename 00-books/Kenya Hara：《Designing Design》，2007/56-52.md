Looking back, I see that since it was not premised on practical commercial production, it was a bit too romantic as a product design show, but I also see there my sincere interest in designing food. The macaroni project arose from my curiosity about whether food can be designed. Cooking might be considered, in a broad sense, as design. However, since it has already been deeply cultivated through a very high level of culture, it doesn't seem that significant to dig around in it by intentionally bringing up the concept of design.
