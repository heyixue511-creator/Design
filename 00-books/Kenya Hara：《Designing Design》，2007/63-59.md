![](images/7c6276f4dee91f5668bcf23ad9bcbd6cddc95fc364fa3fff951834ae099d1963.jpg) ## Kaoru Kasai: OTTOCO The art director Kaoru Kasai participated in this project as a guest. His extremely delicate sensibility is usually occupied in advertising for popular imported spirits and tea. His work, exuding tranquility in every respect, represents one of the sophisticated elements of today's Japanese graphic design. While working towards a design for cylindrical macaroni, he came up with a solution shaped like a classical Japanese dance mask, with a familiar protruding mouth. Kasai says we should imagine the little mouths exhaling the steam of very hot soup. The recipe is Pie Stuffed with Pasta and Carbonara Sauce. Because the pasta is comically shaped, the recipe was meant to baffle our expectations. When the box is opened on the table, the pasta pops up from a pie, and every charming mouth exhales a little puff of steam.
