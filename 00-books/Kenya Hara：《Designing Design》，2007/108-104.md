![](images/59597d5e21bb628a7a111d9a599fa7bfe82bb72456622454291aefdd8b4d13e1.jpg) ## Yasuhiro Suzuki: Cabbage Bowls Let's go back to the haptic designs. The artist YasuhiroSuzuki made cabbages out of paper. He created the shapes by accurately molding real cabbage leaves out of silicone, then reproducing these in paperclay. The leaves are designed as bowls. All of the leaves in a single cabbage have been reproduced, so Suzuki's reconstituted cabbage weighs almost as much as a real one.
