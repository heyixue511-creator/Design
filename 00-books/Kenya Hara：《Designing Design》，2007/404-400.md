This kind of rest is different from nighttime sleep. It feels great to enjoy fine food and/or a soak in a hot bath, but there are few things better than doing nothing. This is where this team discovered the “bud of resort." This is to take for granted doing nothing while the sun is high in the sky. Furthermore, they found more value in doingit outdoors. “Sleeping outside." This unpretentious title summarizes the essence of relaxation for a human being. The team started their research by observing people lying on the grass in the park. They recorded the number of people as the hours passed
