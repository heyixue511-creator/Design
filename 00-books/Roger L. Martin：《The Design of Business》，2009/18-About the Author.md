## About the Author A best-selling author, Roger Martin is dean of the Rotman School of Management at the University of Toronto as well as a senior adviser to CEOs of large global companies. He is a columnist for BusinessWeek Online’s Innovation and Design Channel and a regular contributor to the Financial Times Judgment Call column and to the Washington Post On Leadership blog. In 2007, BusinessWeek named him one of the ten most influential business professors in the world.
