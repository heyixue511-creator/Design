![](images/e8921ec7e71bc4f9f5797c4fbd504ff551254859eea85544bde454520f42916e.jpg) ## Norihide Imagawa: SHE & HE Norihide Imagawa is an expert in structural design. He's the one international architects very often commission as a partner on projects they do in Japan. Imagawa's macaroni has a convex-concave mesh on one side and is flat on the other, so that each side has a different surface area. As the expansion rate of one side gains advantage over the other when it is boiled, the macaroni warps. Shaped like a pair of icons, one female, one male, the piece warps to suggest some eroticism. The recipe Kogure created for this macaroni is Seafood Soup and Pasta. The SHE & HE pasta is poured into a hodgepodge of seafood and tomatoes, mirroring society itself, with its mix of people and all kinds of things bustling and jostling about together. It's a miniature version of society, with the pasta vitalized only as it warps.
