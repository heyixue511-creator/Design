In 2002, the Japan Design Committee held an exhibition entitled "Optimum (An Original Form of Design)."Because the original form is the optimum, or most favorable, committee members selected products that precisely monitored every aspect of the human environment, in the same way that a living creature evolves in adjusting to its environment. The white of paper allows for highly flexible usage, becoming a floor, a wall, a platform, or a space with depth. ![](images/4e979eee42d0fa9b75eb00fd8e2b9fd4f5d3bee661516ec23ee455dfdb673915.jpg)
