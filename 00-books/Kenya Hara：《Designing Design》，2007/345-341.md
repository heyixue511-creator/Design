The motifs in the calendar are from the Honzo Zusetsu, a series of illustrated natural history books authored by Shunzan Takagi about two centuries ago. The viewpoint is not scientific and analytical, but celebratory of the awe due to nature. This viewpoint is inaccordance with the original concept of Expo 2005. I intuitively felt that these illustrations were a perfect fit for the imagery to convey the concept of the coming Expo. Some may wonder about the use of antiquities for a future-oriented event.
