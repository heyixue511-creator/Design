![](images/cef53c38d6d845e5172a9554f5eb72b5ac6fe2f6df8626c4874c1d34123b38e7.jpg) MUJI's 2004 advertising campaign was launched with the keyword and concept, "house." We used the word as the copy for the ad, but in keeping with the MUJI concept, it didn't convey a particular or clear message. It was simply a vehicle for emptiness. It functioned as a focal point for thought. When I think about it, I realize that we Japanese have managed for a very long time without any particular criteria or standards for creating our own style of housing. More than 100 years have passed since, in pursuit of Westernization and modernization, we turned a cold shoulder to traditional Japanese housing. But we still have no specific form of living space that we can boast of to the rest of the world. What today's Japan can do, having reached the point of no return vis-a-vis the past, is challenge this ambiguous status quo by asking, “How should we live in the postmodern era?"Although we made a grievous error by interrupting the ecological cycle of nature, we have been concentrating our efforts to restore it: once-polluted rivers have survived and salmon have returned to spawn. It's been a long time since we realized that there was a girdle of contradictions around cities that can be described as beautiful. Japan is not greatly blessed with natural resources. And so we have been able to become even more conscious, and at an earlier stage, of the limitations of the earth's resources, and have practiced moderation and contrived technological methods to help sustain the environment. In this country, under these circumstances, what kind oflifestyle or living conditions should we aim for? At the height of Japan's economic boom, our urban housing met with harsh criticism: our living spaces were called rabbit hutches. But we may have access to a particular rationality precisely because of the difficulties we face. In these circumstances, we can freely plan our own housing style, not talking about dreams, but as dictated by our actual situations. Affluence may be the discovery of rational housing styles that are perfectly compatible with our individuallifestyles.
