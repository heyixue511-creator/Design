The plotted points here are Chernoff faces, which reduce well, maintaining legibility even with individual areas of .o5 square inches as shown.3 The analyst would observe the standard X-Y scatterplot and then turn to the within-scatter detail, seeking clusters of similar observations over the X-Y plane.
