What is the simplicity that acts as the backdrop for the MUJI concept?What's good about simplicity? How is simplicity related to the quality of a product? The 2005 ad campaign touched on these themes. At the same time, they helped delve into the origin of Japanese aesthetics, which look for beauty within simplicity. In the photo is shown a national treasure, Togudo's Dojinsai, at Kyoto's Jisho-ji (temple). Dojinsaiis the origin of both the teahouse and the space today considered a traditional Japanese-style room.
