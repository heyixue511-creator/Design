## 9780486136240 This Dover edition, first published in 1979, is an unabridged republication of the work as published by the Solomon R. Guggenheim Foundation for the Museum of Non-Objective Painting, New York City, in 1947, in a translation by Howard Dearstyne and Hilla Rebay, edited and prefaced by Hilla Rebay. The work was originally published in 1926 as Punkt und Linie zu Fläche, the ninth in a series of fourteen Bauhaus books edited by Walter Gropius and L. Moholy-Nagy.
