Here the problem consists in maintaining the "horizontal" unity of the melodic lines against the "vertical" harmonic coherence of the chords. This is achieved by keeping the melodic lines as simple and consistent as the musical task permits. For the progression from one chord to the next this means, for example, the use of grouping by "similarity of location."
