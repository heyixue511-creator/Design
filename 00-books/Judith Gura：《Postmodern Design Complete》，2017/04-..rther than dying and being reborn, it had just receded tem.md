I have painted Postmodernism with a broad brush, including designs that were deliberately postmodern, and others simply influenced by it; designers in the movement's mainstream, and others who occasionally explored the territory. This inclusiveness, and the accompanying images, makes the point that Postmodernism consistently overflowed its defined boundaries. No architect, artist, or designer remained untouched.
