## A note on the design of Information

There is no universally established methodology in the field of design; it is a living body and new elements are added from time to time, Sometimes a new look at the world of science or technology, or sometimes a new insight or a political situation has provoked new thinking and methods to be invented and subseguently introduced into teaching. One of thesecases is the way of looking at the product as a means of communication(Theodor Ellinger, 1966) that might have had its roots in the then upcoming theory of Semantics. This view spurred a lot of thinking and had practical implications because if the product is a means of communication it has to rely heavily on its design-its form, shape, and structure-in order to express its communicative qualities. Design activity now is not merely a question of form, it is more important to design the context of the product, which wants to be on stage and made public. Instead of “How are things made?”comes the question “What does it mean to us?”(Bürdek) and this opens many new horizons for the design and demands special qualifications from the designer, making him a “steward of thedisciplines”(CarnegieMellon University, USA).

These developments include the move away from a established design attitude to an anti-movement to functional design, the development of product language (Offenbach), the new challenge of microelectronics, in which function no longer comes before form. “Form follows function”, Sullivan's famous saying, which has been a rock of functional design, is no longer any help. Electronics can be packaged in any shape and this characteristic demanded a new approach.

Postmodern design, though of very specific Western origin, swept like a mighty wave across the World and became merely a style, forgetting its original intentions. The discovery of emotional qualities opened the door to many subjective interpretations like “Form follows emotion”.

在设计领域，并不存在放之四海而皆准的方法。设计本身充满着活力，新的元素一直都在不断被注入其中。有时候，科技世界的新发展、新的洞察力或者新的政治形势，都会引发新的思维方式或方法的出现，进而在教学过程中被使用。其中一种例子就是把产品当作一种交流方式的看法(西奥多·艾琳格，1966)，其在随后出现的语义学体系当中找到了理论依据。这种观点引发了许多人的思考，并具有实用的现实意义，因为如果产品成为一种交流的方式，就必须强烈依赖于其本身的设计状况：外观、形状以及结构等，以便于传达产品的相关信息。当今时代，设计活动已经不只关注于外在形态的问题，而更关注于产品的内涵，而这才是需要被大力宣传和推广的部分。以往类似“东西是如何制造出来的”这样的问题已被“这东西对我们来说意味着什么”所取代(布尔德克)。而这个问题为设计开启了更多全新的视角，同时对设计师们也提出了更高的要求，他们要成为“多学科的全能战士”(美国卡内基梅隆大学)。

这些领域的发展包括从既有的设计思维与态度向功能化设计的反向转化，产品语言的发展(奥芬巴赫设计学院)，微电子领域的新挑战等等，这其中都存在一个共同点，即外表比功能更重要。沙利文的名言“形式追随功能”，在过去曾被视为功能化设计的金玉良言，如今已经派不上任何用场。电子产品可以被包装成各式各样的形状，而这种特性需要使用一种新方法来审视。

虽然其起源于西方世界，后现代设计的浪潮已经席卷全球各地，成为一种风格，而其原本之初衷却早已被人忘却。设计中情感特征的发现使得对“形式追随功能”之类的主观话语的阐释有了一种全新的解读。

A lot could besaid about the many attitudes, philosophies, andtheories that have originated from this new look at the product and its communicative function, about the opening of design to related discíplines and the number of design activities that have sprung up as a result, like corporate identity, event design, exhibition design, network design, home page design, or visualisation design-to name only a few of the activities that have become established professional services.

In my exercises, I want the problem to be boileddown to simple questions. In the design of information, where you are dealing not only with hardware elements,but with their structure and their perceptual qualities, with what you want to communicate, and with what the product's meaning should be. To accomplish your taskyou can use the structure of communication, hierarchical intentions, practical elements, and the shape, form, and texture of the product to express and make it understood. Think of a mobile telephone, a stereo installation, a digital camera, or an alarm clock, which all require understandable operations.

## The Information Design Exercise

This exercise is intended to train thinking that goes beyond a mechanical function. It aims to help you discover new ways of information transfer and their incorporation in the design of a new productstructure.

Choose an electronic device that yoù know confidently. Try to make it understandable by information design or invent a new communication structure which may result in a better product.

A sequence of questions can be helpful:

—What is the informative task of the product to be designed?(General briefing)

—Think about the hierarchy of information, what is more, what is less important?(Hierarchicaltree)

—Which information options do you have-verbal, visual, analogue, digital, symbolic? Which material options do you have-surface, colour, shape, proportion, and others(Listofoptions)

这种针对产品和其交流功能的全新视角，产生了众多相关的思维方式、态度与理论体系。设计领域对相关学科和如火如荼的设计活动的开放步伐也在逐步加快，如企业形象设计、大型活动设计、展会设计、网络设计、主页设计或可视化设计等等，目前这些设计类活动已经发展成为成熟的专业化服务，相关的值得讨论的内容相当之丰富。

在本书的这部分练习中，我想将要讨论的问题浓缩成若干个简单的小问题。在信息设计部分里，你将会处理硬件元素，包括其结构和感性特征，同时还可以添加期望进行交流的元素，以及产品的内涵意义等。为了顺利完成任务，你可以使用沟通的基本架构、多层级意愿、实际元素，以及可以展现产品特征信息的相关方面，如形状、形式以及质地等。你可以尝试使用以下参照物：手机、立体声播放系统、数码相机或者闹钟等。它们的操作说明都应该达到“易懂”的标准。

## ·信息设计练习

该练习旨在训练不受机械功能限制进行思维的能力。它会帮助你发现信息转换的新方向，以及它们与新产品结构设计之间的融合。

选择一种你非常熟知的电子设备。试着通过信息设计或创造一种全新的交流结构(可能会产生一种更好的产品)来让别人对这种产品更加了解。

提出一系列问题可能会产生很大的帮助：

—将要设计的这种产品的信息化任务是什么?(简要情况介绍)

—思考信息的层级模式，哪一种信息更重要，哪一种不太重要？(层级树)

你拥有哪方面的信息要素？（言语、视觉、类比、数码或象征类)你拥有什么样的材料要素?(表面、色彩、形状、比例亦或其他)(设计选择项列表)
