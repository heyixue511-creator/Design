# 90s ARCHITECTURE & One legacy of Derek Walker's professorship was the division of Architecture and Interior Design into two separate courses running in parallel, a programme that was continued under Theo Crosby. When Dinah Casson took over as Course Director in 1993, she brought the two together by creating a core course – Architecture and Interior Design. Nigel Coates's appointment as Professor in 1995 radically changed the direction of the course. Renowned as the enfant terrible of British architecture, but well-respected as a teacher at the Architectural Association, Coates brought to the course a vitality and spirit that has made it one of the most notable and distinctive architectural courses in the world.
