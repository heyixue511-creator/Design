# Design Research Society Conference

Quantum leap: managing new product innovation. UCE, 8–10 September, 1998

This is a Design Research Society event at the University of Central England in collaboration with Bradford Management Centre. Other collaborators will include DTI, the Design Council and the Chartered Society of Designers.

## Background

Research into innovation shows that change is often an aspect of personal vision. This according to CBT/DTI studies shows that such enthusiasm for change has the ability to unlock potential and eventually invert the organisational pyramid. It is particularly important therefore to develop the aspects of design that can be represented by empowered designers in a strategic alliance between research, design and management. It is particularly timely to debate the power of such strategic relationships. The conference, initiated at the University of Bradford Management Centre is planned to be held at the University of Central England, in Britain's manufacturing heartland.

## Aim

To build collaborative relationships between the following communities: business, design and research.

## Objectives

Investigate the management of product innovation to promote economic competitiveness. Encourage new initiatives and joint ventures between research, design and business communities. Provide a forum for discussion and dissemination of research findings and industrial practice.
