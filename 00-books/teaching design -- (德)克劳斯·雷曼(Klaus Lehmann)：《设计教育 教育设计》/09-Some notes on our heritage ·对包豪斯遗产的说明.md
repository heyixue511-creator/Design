## Some notes on our heritage

The heritage of the Bauhaus is to be found in modern design teaching everywhere. The workshop, or laboratory, a central part of Bauhaus teaching, was the playground for experiment in the acquisition of manual skills and played a central role for generating new ideas. It is still a part of our educational system.

Asked about what was the moststimulating element in their studies, former students always name, alongside their individual teacher, the workshop as the moststimulating field for innovation and thevital contribution to their personal development. Modern media have naturally changed the creative process, but the reliance on the computer as the sole tool seems to me to be an impoverishment and represents a grave loss. Modern research on the workings of the brain andon the nature of creativity in general seem to underline this position and the demand for a new practical theory of education. That is a great challenge. My personal teaching experience leads me to believe that the workshop or laboratory, where the learner works with materials, tools, and machines, cannotbe replaced by the computer. “Hands on”is a vital part of artistic education.

Of course it is important what you teach, but that will soon be outdated by technological progress, by changes in consumer behaviour, or by fashions, which all become obsolete very fast, even within a single generation.

## ·对包豪斯遗产的说明

包豪斯的艺术遗产在当今设计教学中随处可见。包豪斯教学方法的核心内容在艺术工作室或实验室中进行的动手技能训练与实验中都得到了应用，并且它还不断被发展改造，催生出更新颖的点子与理念。它依然是艺术教学系统中不可缺少的部分。

当被问及在研究过程中，什么东西最令人兴奋时，以前的学生们都会说，除了自己的老师之外，工作室就是最令人兴奋的地方，因为这是创新的基地，是提供个人发展动力的加油站。现代媒体已经从本质上改变了创新的过程，然而对我来说，对电脑的过分依赖把电脑作为设计创新的唯一工具，是一种倒退的表现，对整个行业来说则意味着不可估量的损失。整体上来说，现代关于大脑的运作机制以及创新的本质研究似乎都指出了这一点，整个业界都期待一种全新的实用教育理论的产生。这是极大的挑战。我个人的教学经历让我相信，学习者们在工作室或实验室使用原料、工具和机器进行创造的过程，是仅使用电脑所不可替代的。艺术教育中学生一定要“勤动手”。

## ·关于艺术教学的总结

教学的内容的确非常重要，但随着科技发展，消费者行为的改变，以及时尚潮流的推进，它会逐渐变得老套而过时，知识更新的周期有时短得超乎想象，甚至只需要一代人的时间。

思维方式比教授的内容更重要，因为它可以让你掌握应对问题的方法、技能和心态。

换句话说，教学的方式也同等重要在学生看来，老师把他们当 作独立的个体，并要求他们积极配合教学工作。在西方国家的教育领 域，教学都是互动的，激励学生们树立个人目标，在学习过程中激发学 goals, motivating the student's own ambitions in the processof learning, and reminding him of his social responsibility in the process of developing his personality. Its goal is, first of all, todevelop creativity andself-fulfilment. Given this, the professional skills will be acquired quite naturally along with, hopefully,the ethics of hisprofession.

In teaching, methodology and skills are mostly of secondary importance to a student. But the person who teaches you, who is able to stimulate your mind, whom you trust and consider competent, who looks at you as an individual with care and sympathy, is a invaluable element for personal development and learning.

"Teachers are the underestimated software of educational and cultural development."(K.L.)

生奋进，并在个人性格的发展过程中告知他们担负起一定的社会责任。教学的目的就在于鼓励创新，让自我变得更加充实。基于这一点，教师将专业技能和职业道德一起传授给学生，一切都是那么顺其自然。

教学过程中，对于学生来说，方法和技能的重要性是居于第二位的。然而作为教师，如果他能激发学生的灵感，赢得学生的信任和爱戴，被学生看作是能力、包容和博爱的化身，那么他就是学生个人学习成长过程中最有价值的组成部分了。
