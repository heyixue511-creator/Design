# ACKNOWLEDGEMENTS This book began as a history of the Kelmscott Press; approximately two years ago I realised that much of the material I had gathered in my notes belonged more appropriately in a bibliography than in a history of the Press, and I accordingly began to reorganise the notes with that end in view. I should add that I still hope to write the history.
