In making this appeal to you, I will not suppose that I am speaking to any who refuse to admit that we who are part of civilization are responsible to posterity for what may befall the fairness of the earth in our own days, for what we have done, in other words, towards the progress of Architecture; if any such exist among cultivated people, I need not trouble myself about them; for they would not listen to me, nor should I know what to say to them.
