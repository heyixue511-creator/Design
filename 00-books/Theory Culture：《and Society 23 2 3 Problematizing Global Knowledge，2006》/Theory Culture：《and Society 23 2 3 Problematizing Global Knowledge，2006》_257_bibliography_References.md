Pnina Werbner is Professor of Social Anthropology in the School of Criminology, Education, Sociology and Social Work at Keele University. Her recent books include Pilgrims of Love: The Anthropology of a Global Sufi Cult (Indiana University Press, 2004) and Imagined Diasporas among Manchester Muslims (James Currey Publishers, 2002).
