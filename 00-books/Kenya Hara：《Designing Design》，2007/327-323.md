![](images/dc06ec2bae78b1b21fb0ce01469d90fce9b8b0a80f8fa899f3b64b927a6ac361.jpg) "Washbasin" placed at spa, Mukayu, 2006. This washbasin is a modern interpretation of the traditional installation for washing the hands and purifying the spirit. Water droplets swiftly roll along the water-repellent plate and are conducted toward the large, shallow funnel-shaped plate, eventually spiraling into the square hole in its center at a leisurely pace. The installation is designed to help viewers appreciate the purity of water and attain peace of mind. ![](images/9c5d908fe1bbd26c13bcc8fa5b55576457f3b1ea7982c2655b19188437b9dc39.jpg) ![](images/0259b0156689f0d943f7acc5f6f929a5d14e8b442759ccf7593cb4d927ac6935.jpg) VIEWING THE WORLD FROM THE TIP OF ASIA
