## lll. ![](images/7f7dc2f8d74abaed241d4b6860a1aca6bfc128e6fc028b4036dfbd5af6904eaa.jpg) ## EARTH, WATER AND AIR. Symbols of the static area are plummet (position) and balance. The plummet aims at the earth center where all materially-bound existence is anchored. But there are regions with different laws and new symbols, signifying freer movement and dynamic position. Water and atmosphere are transitional regions. ![](images/8f68f7ad2d60e1f5f27ac8a7f84aef45e211eb86637df40a2a3433fa15e7f939.jpg)
