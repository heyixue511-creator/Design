This is no virtual visual phenomenon but a ripple-like visual effect occurring in real, tangible space. The guidance signage is set three dimensionally against the backdrop of the louvered walls. By virtue of floatingin space, the 3-D arrows gain true indicative function, as they point us in 3-D directions. Please note the reality of the arrows that actually turn corners, or those that seem to be thrust directly into the wall above the restroom doors. The identity of this museum comes from a combination of the visual effect of ripple-like movement occurring in rhythm with the architecture, and the arrows drifting in layered space.
