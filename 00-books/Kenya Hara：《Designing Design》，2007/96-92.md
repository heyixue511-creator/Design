![](images/d78274f4efc2e4fda27f3d6d1c1b4ab7d916720c8eea82175f6b235abb2ed4b7.jpg) ## Naoto Fukasawa:Juice Skin These fruit juice packages are the work of the product designer Naoto Fukasawa. As his design is very easy to understand, it probably needs no interpretation from me. The banana juice package is particularly superb. For this, Fukasawa used the familiar Tetra Prisma® Aseptic package. The gentle angles of the packaging reveal something in common with the feeling of holding a banana. When a stem is added to the flap, it can't be seen as anything but a banana. Fukasawa has dexterously edited only the unique features of his subject.
