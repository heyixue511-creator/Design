2. For this chapter I  will refer to the underground railway network of London simply as the ‘Underground’. Over time, it has been variously the ‘Underground group’, London Passenger Transport Board, London Transport and Transport for London. In its present form it also comprises the London Overground system and will soon also feature Crossrail 1, a long-distance railway which will serve existing Underground stations and new interchanges of a scale unprecedented in London.
