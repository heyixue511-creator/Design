# DESIGNING ![](images/5b3499e0c56c5be969a37011b4c77121da63079be21774241f42170c5f5648f8.jpg) Paragraphic Books, New York ## FOR PEOPLE We bear in mind that the object being worked on is going to be ridden in, sat upon, looked at, talked into, activated, operated, or in some other way used by people individually or en masse. When the point of contact between the product and the people becomes a point of friction, then the industrial designer has failed.
