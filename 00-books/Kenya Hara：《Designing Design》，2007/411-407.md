## Exformation Continues to Evolve As of 2006, the theme of the third year of exformation is “creases," which are not fresh, plump and full of life, but the opposite. Normally creases have a negative image, but upon reflection, we realized that there's a way to think about things that will allow us to discover their positive value. For example, the best jeans of all are not new, but worn-out jeans, either bleached and weathered by machine washing, or simply vintage jeans. We expect to be able to exform something by concentrating on the concept and the phenomenon of creases, and the project continues to make headway.
