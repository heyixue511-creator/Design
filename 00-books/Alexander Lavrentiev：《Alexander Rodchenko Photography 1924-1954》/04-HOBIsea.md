Appendix Anhang Appendice ## 1891 Alexander M. Rodchenko is born on 5 December in St. Petersburg. His father, Mikhail Mikhailovich Rodchenko (1852–1907), is a theatre props master; his mother, Olga Yevdokimovna (1865–1933), is a laundress. ## 1911-1914 Studies at the Kazan School of Art, where he meets Varvara Fedorovna Stepanova (1894–1958). ## 1916 Called up for military service. Mayakovsky. ## 1923-1925 Film work. Rodchenko designs the film titles for the magazine Kinopravda. ## 1924 Rodchenko starts taking photographs (portraits of Mayakovsky, N. Aseyev, his mother, and others). ## 1925 Birth of his daughter, Varvara.
