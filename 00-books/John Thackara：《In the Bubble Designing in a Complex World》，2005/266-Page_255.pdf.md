The platform is based on the principle that people with a chronic disease are often best placed to manage it. ‘‘Know-how and advice has to be close at hand for people to draw on where and when it’s most appropriate. Expert patients and carers could become peer-to-peer providers of support,’’ they conclude. Hilary Cottam and Charles Leadbeater, Health: Co-creating Services (London: Design Council, 2004).
