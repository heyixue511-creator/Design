When she told me that she designs texture not for clothing or bags, but for its own sake, I finally understood her vision. So I suggested we have a thin, bone-like table produced to maintain the purity of the texture she had designed. We asked a product designer to help with the practical design of the table. It's so thin it looks as if only the surfaces of the table are floating in the air. The name Gazelle probably refers to this thin, fragile presence.
