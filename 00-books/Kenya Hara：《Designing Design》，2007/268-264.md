The photographer was again Tamotsu Fujii, who shot the Horizon ad campaign in the previous year. We searched for powerful scenes that would say, "People live alive." We wanted to find the reality of houses not designed by architects. We felt that if we considered the concept of house surrounded only by familiarity, our ideas might deteriorate.
