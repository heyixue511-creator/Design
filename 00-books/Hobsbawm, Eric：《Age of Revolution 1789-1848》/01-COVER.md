THE AGE OF REVOLUTION 1789-1848 ![](images/505d9ea789a24cada7b7339ef74e298e2401db64aabd6df006c89224d19f5f95.jpg) ERIC HOBSBAWM HISTORY OF CIVILIZATION Eric Hobsbawm was born in Alexandria in 1917 and was educated in Vienna, Berlin, London and Cambridge. A Fellow of the British Academy and the American Academy of Arts and Sciences, with honorary degrees from universities in several countries, he taught until retirement at Birkbeck College, University of London, and since then at the New School for Social Research in New York.
