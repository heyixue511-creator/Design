![](images/467ab722e9554cbb0d4898f6a68f625487438d243745df59fc281957766edcd9.jpg) ## Atelier Zo:MACCHERONI The studio Atelier Zo is known for building architecture that radiates human warmth. Staying aloof from cold modernism and avoiding the clash of harried hours, they do their job at their own pace and with their own sensibilities in the bosom of tranquil nature. Their macaroni proposal gets its twisted shape from the human hand: Form a piece of dough into a bunch and quickly twist it perpendicularly. Voilà. This design brings tomind a picnic with family or friends, enjoying one another's company, the appearance of the fresh pasta in a big dish raising our expectations. They say anyone can make this shape. The recipe for Tortellini Pomodoro is a genuine Italian dish, but the roughly hand-shaped macaroni seems typically Japanese.
