The future is not where everyone looks for it, "there," right after the present. It's not an integral number in a line: 9, 10, 11.... It's somewhere like 6.8 or 7.3. The new design scene nestled in ordinary life will be created by the intellect that perceives the unlimited numbers between any two successive integers. After the RE-DESIGN Exhibition was held in four Japanese cities, it was sent off to art ![](images/a3fa46393457e0e3e6fa1023572698ed38169b9b140d5cbe890f9f776b820d67.jpg) galleries in other parts of the world, with a bit of a push from our side. The invitations were sparse at first, but it finally got into the world tour mode, andas it debuted in various cities, requests began to pour in. Over a period of several years, RE-DESIGN visited Glasgow, Copenhagen, Hong Kong, Toronto, Shanghai, Beijing and Shenzhen. Contrary to my expectations, the public response was huge. In the beginning this anything-but-ordinary exhibition was misunderstood as something humorous.
