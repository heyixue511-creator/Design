## 441. 'Spectator'section, Arena, No.2, Spring 1987

'Spectator', sited at the front of the magazine, carries both critical review and overview. The section uses the same typeface for its headline as the front cover logo, and the indentation of quotes set in Kabel brings further movement to the pages. The reversed out 'A' together with the solid bar beneath the headline is a parallel to the corporate 'flag' on the cover of The Face.
