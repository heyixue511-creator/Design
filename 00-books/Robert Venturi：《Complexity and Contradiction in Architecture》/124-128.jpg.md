Our approach was to wrap around rather than add to, the existing buff brick building with new interior spaces on the back and the north side and with a new detached wall in front which contains a court in its residual space. The old building is covered over but modified as little as possible for economy. The wraparound wall, in its big scale and dark brick material increases the unity of Main Street. Through the big, grilled openings in the front outer wall is glimpsed the older, lighter, small-scale building so that its architecture is respected. From close-up the new is juxtaposed upon the old.
