![](images/2fe2645887a72b9d4f8f1232a9b505e8783c8b61b03beaabb27afd2d394aa8ed.jpg) # MUJI Nothing,Yet Everything Visualizing the MUJI Concept Since the 2002 advertisement campaign, I've been both MUJI's art director and a member of its advisory board, helping to create its vision. These two jobs differ slightly in content and in my position, but coincide in the visualization of the MUJI concept. Born into the Japanese market in the 1980s, the original product concept was rather naïve. I believe that it is my role to communicate in such a way as to empower that concept to meet today's global context, in which a wiser way of dealing with manufacturing, resources and the environment has begun to arise. No longer a localized experiment, MUJI is now a practical project operating within the global consumer culture.
