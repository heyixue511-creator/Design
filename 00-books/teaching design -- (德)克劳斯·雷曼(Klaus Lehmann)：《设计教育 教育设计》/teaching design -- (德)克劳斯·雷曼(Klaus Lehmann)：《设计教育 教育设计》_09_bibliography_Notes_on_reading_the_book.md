# Notes on reading the book

The core of my book is about basic design, about teaching, general thoughts, and cultural implications. It contains exercises that I have thought up during my life in teaching and practised in many countries anddesign institutions. It should be understood that I always startwith a practicaldesign problem, explaining why the exercise is of use, andhow it is embedded in an industrial and cultural process-why we are doing it. The next step asks for open experiments, which are encouraged and discussed-learning by doing. The final step is a comparison of different results to a narrow problem-reflecting on what has been learned-and the development of general criteria through personal experience.

The part with exercises consists of 6chapters in which I try to cover relevant aspects of tacklingdesign problems. It should beunderstood that I have developed these exercises not from the usual basic design teaching,widely known from the Bauhaus and itsfollowers, but in relation to practical problems encountered in designing and where | have found a need for practical and fundamental help.

I have designed this part of my book on different levels: for fast or for slow reading:

—In the introductory notes to the exercises, I relate the exercise to its ideological and practical background.

—The description of the exercise is a teaching element, in which one or several criteria are investigated. These should become part of a basic vocabulary, bringing a problem to the point, investigating not only the formal, the instrumental, but also the personal knowledge. For this reason, functional requirements are omitted.

—I describe different possible approaches and give didactic hints.

—With occasional quotations, set in italics, I describe students' reactions.

—By recommending further reading I want to stimulate the reader's own studies by quoting relevant literature and suggesting directions for research.

本书的核心内容是关于基础设计、教学、一般性思考以及文化含义等。主要包括我在世界各国和各个设计学校所教授以及实践过程中所归纳的练习。有一点必须强调的是，我总是以一个实践中的设计问题作为开端，并解释使用这种练习的原因，以及它们如何在工业和文化的发展进程中发挥作用，即利用它们的原因。接下来是关于鼓励开放性实验以及相关讨论的内容，即在动手中学习。最后一步是将解决某一具体问题所做出的不同的实验结果之间的比较，即对学习过的内容，以及从个人经验中总结的普遍性标准的发展情况进行思考。

共有六个章节包含练习部分，在这部分我将探讨关于应对设计问题的相关话题。需要说明的是，我并不是基于广泛使用的包豪斯以及其追随者所推崇的基础设计教学模式来编写这些练习的，而是在实际设计操作过程中遇到问题后，我感觉学生们可能需要关于基础或实践方面的帮助，因此才开始了编写工作。

为了方便读者快速浏览或细致阅读，我将本书的练习部分分割成以下不同层次：

——在练习的人门说明中，我将简要介绍练习的意识形态以及实践操作的相关背景信息。

——关于练习的描述是一种教学要素，其中将探索出一套或几套标准。这些应该成为基础知识的一部分，针对其中的某一点提出问题，对形式上的、有关工具的，以及个人收集的知识进行查验与核实。由于以上原因，功能要求可以被忽略。

——我将描述不同种类可能的研究方法，并给出若干有关教学的提示。

——偶尔我会使用斜体引言的形式来描述学生们的反应。

—通过引申阅读书籍的推荐，我将选取相关的文献，以及对研究方向提出建议，以激发读者的研究热情。
