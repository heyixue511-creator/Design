Was borne at Exeter in Devon, the descended, both by Father and Mother, of Worshipfull parentage. By my Fathers side, from an antient familie of Bodley, or bodleigh,. of Dunscombe by Crediton ; and by my Mother from Robert Hone Esq; of ottereySaint Marie, nine Milles from Exon. My Father in the time of Queene Marie, beinge knowne and noted to be an eneinie to Poperie, was so cruelly threatned, and so narrowlie observed, by those that malliced his religion, that for the safegarde of himselfe, and my Mother, who was wholly affected as my Father, he knew no waye so secure, as to flie into Garmanie: B 2 Where 343.
