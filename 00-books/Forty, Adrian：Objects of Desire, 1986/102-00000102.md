Singer 'New Family' sewing machine, 1858. After the unsuccessful 'Family' machine, Singer quickly introduced a new model for the domestic market. \*Quoted in Brandon, p. 126. Squirrel sewing machine, 1858. Another, more fanciful attempt to domesticate the sewing machine. The squirrel was chosen because of the creature's frugality and providence.
