Layed out, they look like upended Petri dishes at some biological research institute. They bring to mind spilt water swelled with surface tension. Tadpole-like patterns can be seen inside. Sobue once told me the story of a friend's shocking experience, a story that freezes the senses. He had been taking a summer nap, but was awakened by an itching on his elbow.
