![](images/72a3582d2f6f325dfc380f5e47ca1eba0235291ed51fed5cc1639d073bcc51dc.jpg) ## Kosuke Tsumura: Kami Tama The fashion designer Kosuke Tsumura designed some lanterns. After we asked the creators to participate, we gave them information about several new technologies applicable to design and which we'd already conducted some research into. Tsumura chose the hair implant technology and applied it to traditional Japanese lanterns in cooperation with a wig maker. Because the paper used for traditional lanterns isn't strong enough to support implants without tearing, Tsumura pasted a reinforcing silk mesh base onto the paper
