Paul Renner invited Tschichold to Munich in 1927 to teach calligraphy and typography at the Master School for German Book Printers, and Tschichold remained there until 1933. Among the design commissions he undertook in Munich was a series of film posters for the movie theater Phoebus Palast in 1927.
