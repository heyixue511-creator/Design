•G19 Design for Alphabet Peter Behrens Upper-case alphabet in Roman script, with the words "diese machen Worte zeichnen" underneath in Behrens's handwriting. The initials for the AEG trademark were taken from this alphabet (see G80), and it was used in numerous versions in brochures, posters, advertisements, and inscriptions. (A page of pencil sketches in the Behrens papers show early studies, and the note "poliphili" points to the model for this script: the "Hypnerotomachia Poliphili" of Aldus Manutius, Venice, 1499. Technique Pencil on parchment. Dimensions 19 × 26.5 cm; height of letters 2 cm.
