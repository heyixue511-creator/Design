However , although it was far from u nknown in the Ming period for the n ames of famous scholars , artists or officials to be attached to books by unscrupulous publishers, the assumption has been made here that these men, all of whom save one were senior to the author and most of whom like him were resident in the province o f Southern Zhili (the modern Jiangsu), did consent to the appearance of their names in the published version of Treatise on Superfluous Things.
