This team began its research with this simple statement of intuition. Truly, as soon as the cone is in our hands, we forget the hustle and bustle of reality; we are instantly transported into “soft-serve ice cream time."It might be the transient nature of soft-serve—that delectable treat that is more ephemeral than ice cream—that attracts us. The moment of tasting a cone of cold soft-serve ice cream is sweetness itself. It's short-lived, but there are very few things that can transport us to the very doorstep of the “resort" of everyday life so dexterously as this one does.
