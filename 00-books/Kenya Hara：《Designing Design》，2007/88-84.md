![](images/3cb3efdcb2bfae7c94270e1e31e9ba80efe667b80826023af5362e5a62921e76.jpg) ## Toyo Ito: ## High Five with a Hand from the Future—Gel Doorknob The architect Toyo Ito designed a doorknob. It's made out of a very soft material called“hydro-gel." Conventional doorknobs are hard, but Ito's goes "squish" when you grasp it. Ito's architectural work has always tried to represent architecture in a sort of abstract manner, by eliminating tangibility asfar as is practical. For instance, Ito thinks walls don't need to have massive materiality. A wall could be something thin, a membrane that partitions space.
