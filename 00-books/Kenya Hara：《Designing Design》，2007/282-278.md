Because a tearoom is a simple space unfixed to any particular location, the consciousness of those who share it for a time becomes so receptive that the smallest bit of ingenuity will engender in their minds the richest image. Here is the origin of the principle of "communication through simplicity"that Rikyu achieved. In this space he created superb performances invoking dazzling images in his guests' minds, performances that attracted the warlords and other powerful characters of the time. Communication executed through the medium of the tea ceremony grew into a well-defined artistic milieu against the backdrop of an era of war. We can say that this is part of the MUJI concept.
