![](images/f4c2b9163ce54b33b19659ffaf346a43b6210a158933af59aa6bec33ac4ae4d8.jpg) The exformation project themes I have not explained here are the following: an oral collection of “resort seeds" discovered in everyday life; research into creating a harmonious, mirth-invoking image by writing in the simpler, more rounded phonetic characters known as hiragana words that are normally written in complex, formal kanji (Chinese characters) and in foreign languages; gathering100 scenes involving tea from all over the country, based on the idea that “resort" can be found in tea; research on the hot spring resort town of Atami, which has been on the decline, but whose identity as an orthodox resort has been motivated. Exformation taught us that the elements that liberate our senses are innumerable, and they are not to be found in extraordinary extravagance, but in everyday life, where the essence of repose is concealed. People subconsciously desire this essence. We also found out that we had been unaware of this truth.
