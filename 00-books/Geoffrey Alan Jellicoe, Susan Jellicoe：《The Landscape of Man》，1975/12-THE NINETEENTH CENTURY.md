The two great personalities who stand out for their influence on landscape design are Thomas Jefferson and F. L. Olmsted. Jefferson, one of the last humanists in the Renaissance tradition, attempted to create an expression of modern liberalism through the media of French neo-classicism, Palladianism and finally classical Rome.
