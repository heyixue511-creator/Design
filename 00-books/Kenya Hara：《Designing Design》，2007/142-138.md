![](images/e3ac4dc28c432576279b78e923f00e045f30b206f8c0332cc3c896a5f65cac0a.jpg) ## Kazunari Hattori: Tailed Gift Cards The art director Kazunari Hattori designed these tailed gift cards. This also unites paper with the wig maker's hair-implant technology. Ribbons are for presents. They give the package a gorgeous appearance and add glitz to gift giving. Tails lend a pretty mysterious impression. What if there were a tail on a present? Artificial fur comes in all kinds of colors and patterns, so you could express yourself with a striped pattern like those of cats' tails, or skunks'. Each of the tails has room for a message on the flip side. The one in the photograph reads “Sorry."
