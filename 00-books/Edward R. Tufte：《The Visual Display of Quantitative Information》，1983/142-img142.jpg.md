Playfair tied the grid to the data in his skyrocketing debt graphic Although the implicit plotting coordinates are based on regular intervals, the vertical grid lines in the published version are irregularly spaced, keyed to significant events. The data-based grid is a shrewd graphical device, serving rather than fighting with the data.
