A gigantic canvas more than100 meters wide appeared on the construction scene facing Ginza Street. Due to strict regulations on temporary enclosures erected in public spaces facing national roads, we were not allowed to express any intention of communication (advertising) on this façade. Therefore, we designed the image of a huge zipper, whose panels were shifted to the right, together with the enclosure, as each stage was completed, making it appearas if the zipper were being unzipped little by little. The zipper is a simile for fashion.
