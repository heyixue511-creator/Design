Josef Bleicher is a member of the Theory, Culture & Society editorial board. His books include: The Hermeneutic Imagination (1982) and Contemporary Hermeneutics (1990). His translation of Georg Simmel’s ‘Kant and Goethe’ will appear in Theory, Culture & Society in 2006. He is an independent scholar, and teaches Goethean science. His current research is on ‘Nature as text: Goethean science as a hermeneutic of nature’.
