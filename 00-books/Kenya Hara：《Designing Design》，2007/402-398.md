Ai observed the situation of vinyl and stripes through photos. Looking at the line of photos, we all felt a deep surge of empathy. At a glance, we understood that colorful vinyl does possess a strange charm, creating a liberating feeling that emancipates the senses. Featured in the photos are a variety of colored sandals, brilliant plastic cups, a blue and white parasol, bright striped tropical fish, and so on. Sometimes the persuasive power of the visual surpasses the eloquence of logic. This presentation was extremely simple and very much to the point. Ai's research went on to simulate images to project the “resort" hidden in vinyl and stripes onto actual scenes of daily life. The series, entitled “Turning Tokyo into Vinyl and Stripes," included such unique objects as a road sign, a shop sign, a motorcycle license plate and a rooftop water tank,
