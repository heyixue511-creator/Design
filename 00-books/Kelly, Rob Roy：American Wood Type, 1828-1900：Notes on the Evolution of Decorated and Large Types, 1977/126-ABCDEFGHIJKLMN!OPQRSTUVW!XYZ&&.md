# ABCDE FGHIJ KLMN! OPQRS TUVW! XYZ&& Antigue Tuscan. First shown by Wells & Webb in their 1849 specimens, without lowercase. Lowercase was first shown in their 1854 catalogue. Lowercase and figures are missing.This design was important in American typographic styling during the nineteenth century. It was especially popular with typefounders from 1860 to the end of the century. It is almost certain that the design originated as a wood type and with the Wells Company.
