## Ⅲ. ## Symbols of form in motion. ![](images/344935f0b61feaddf45fe2539952e1e78faa1cde4535583c577f2a00e4ef3bb9.jpg) The spinning top. A scale, deprived of its material support, and reduced to one point of contact, will topple and fall, even if its weight has been carefully distributed (Fig. 60): ![](images/4296c2f20581c84b634cdf567a51805608c43b3c74439f455439720fff87b0ae.jpg) Horizontal gyration will save this toy from falling. This is the principle of the spinning top. (Fig. 61.) ![](images/efa2ff1eb37c4c0f8d7c24680aa8e5085da5ffdd95056327b4df87bfdf019f7f.jpg) A double top will even dance on a taut string without falling (Fig. 62).
