## ·立方体练习

为了实际体验构成的不同方式，我设计了展现一些不同关注点的练习。如果以组为单位做练习，个人关于研究方法的不同意见都可以拿出来供组员们讨论，学生们可以轻而易举地对彼此的意见作出评估。当然他们会被要求证明自己评估的准确程度，因此他们可以通过这个过程，找出评价的标准。

所有基础练习都旨在培养技能以及总结新标准。

使用完全相同的元素构建一个稳固的正多面体。

这个任务有若干项教学目标。第一，学生们通过研究方法论或实际操作的方式，了解到正多面体可以被分割成三、四、六、八个…不同的部分。

进而，学生们发现在立方体内部存在千差万别的几何关系。通过制作三维纸板模型，学生们发现其中稳固和不稳固的正多面体同时并存，并且他们在几乎不借助外界帮助的情况下，确定了有关形式的初级原则和结构工程学的法则。该练习还表明，对于每个问题的解决方法，都有优雅和不优雅两种。这种练习向世人说明，美学上给人愉悦的成果只能通过结构、形式、材料和制作过程的全程匹配才能达到，而通过这一过程，学生们就可以学到至关重要的原则。

“我意识到，及早到工作室进行学习观察，省去了很多在纸上进行设计的时间与麻烦。通常情况下，问题的解决方案是从材料实验和如何操作的知识储备中获得。”

(朱莉·凡·海顿，一年级学生)

## Bridging exercise

This exercise is also about construction, about structural principles, about developing manual skills and understanding materials. It follows the principle of “learning by doing” as a didactic method and shows that “form follows function”-a principle that has become a substantialcriterion of rational design.

Bridge a given gap with different materials.

## Approaches:

First investigate the properties of your material. Find out what paper, wooden struts, cardboard tubes, or sheet metalcan do. Test the material to discover its best structural performance.

Have a look at bridges, pillars, and beams, at structures in paper, wood, or steel.

Enter into the challenge of bridging the gap with a minimum of material. You will be surprised how many examples you can find in everyday life. In discussing different approaches to thesame given problem, you will discover the vocabulary for the evaluation of form and structure.

" It was a new experience for me to learn that a tooth pick in a model behaves like a real structure.”(Second YearStudent)

Lightweight structures constitute a vital chapter of modern engineering. They are economic, using less material for a given task, and therefore make a contribution to sustainability. Lightweight structures utilize advanced methods of construction and the results of research into materials. By reducing weight, they allow, for instance, greater efficiency in automobile construction, and in the construction of bridges they lead to an economic use of building materials with a transparency that helps integrate them better into their natural surroundings and gives them elegance.

## ·搭桥练习

本练习是关于构成、结构原则、动手技能发展以及关于材料性质的理解。本练习遵循“动手中学习”的教学法原则，并展示了“形式服从于功能”的原则，它已成为合理设计的重要标准之一。

使用不同材料搭桥，跨越既有的沟壑。

方法：

首先要弄清楚材料的特性。找出强度适宜的纸张、木质支柱、纸板三面体或者金属板等，实验中，学生们会发现哪些材料的结构性能更好。

观察桥梁、支柱和横梁的外观，并同时观察纸张、木头或钢材的结构。

随后，他们接受了使用最少量的材料来搭建桥梁的挑战。居然在日常生活中会找到如此多的材料，他们都倍感惊讶。在探讨针对同一问题的不同解决方案时，你会发现，心中已经初步形成了对形式和结构评估机制的框架。

“对我来说，这是一种全新的体验。我感觉使用牙签做的模型就像真实的结构一样。”(一位二年级学生)

轻质结构是现代工程学的重要组成部分之一。它们质优价廉、用料量少，同时还保证了其持久性。轻质结构使用先进的构成方法，并充分利用材料研究的新成果。通过减少重量，它在汽车制造方面的优势更加明显。在桥梁建造方面，它让建筑材料的成本大大降低，使整个建筑与周围的自然环境融为一体，并让建筑本身变得更加优雅美观。

# Note on wrapping, packaging, foldingand cladding (linked together but treated separately for better understanding)

Wrapping is a very elementary process to protect goods and gifts. Apart from its protective function its main function is decorative. Eastern tradition shows a variety of outstanding creative handmade wrapping with leaves, bamboo, strings, or paper. The Japanese book “How to wrap five eggs” has been an eye-opener to the Western design community.

Packaging demands additional functions.Packaging must be manufactured industrially, must be economical in the use of material, must be environmentfriendly, and after use should be returned to the cycle of materials. Food and gifts are usually wrapped in paperor cardboard, but also appliances, coffee machines, computers, hair dryers, and even technicalparts. Packaging has a temporary function and is discarded when its contents have reachedtheir destination. Packaging has become a vital selling point, for it is the first thing the consumer encounters and can be decisive in supporting thedesire to buy its contents. The packaging industry is a huge field for the creative designer.

Folding is a very elementary process, turning a flat piece of material(2 - dimensional) into a spatial structure(3- dimensional). We employ folding when we form a paper plane from a sheet of paper and give it a new function. But there are innumerable examples of new products having been generated by folding. Investigating the many possibilities and approaches, combined here in 2 exercises, is a rewarding fieldof research, which can be carried out using moderate means.

Cladding is another field of practical design, i, e. the encasing of components to give a permanent shape to a product. Usually the casing is made from a durable material; sheet or cast plastics or metal and forms the skin-the durable and permanent shape of the product. Cladding, treated in this way, can open new views of a classical design problem-the work on the shape and its meaning.

For further reading:

Package Design Now, English/French/German, Cologne 2008ISBN 978 -3- 8228-4031-3. A general review of international packaging: Origami.

“包裹”是保护商品和礼物最基本的操作步骤。它除了具有保护功能，还主要具有装饰功能。东方国家的传统中，有多种多样、精妙绝伦的手工制包装形式，如使用叶子、竹子、绳子和纸张等。对于西方设计界人士来说，日本人的一本书《如何包装五个鸡蛋》绝对让他们大开眼界。

“包装”包含了更多功能，也提出了更多要求。“包装”必须通过工业制造进行，在材料的使用上必须方便经济，整个过程必须环保，使用后，相关材料应该可回收，可被循环利用。食物和礼品、家用设备、咖啡机、电脑、吹风机甚至技术设备零件都通常使用纸张或纸板进行包装。“包装”发挥的功能只是暂时性的，当其所包装的物体展现出来时，它的使命也就结束了。“包装”目前已经成为商品至关重要的一项卖点，因为它会给消费者留下第一印象，并左右消费者的购买意愿。“包装”产业是富有创新性的设计师施展才华的广阔空间。

“折叠”也是一种基本的操作过程，即将平整的一片材料(二维)变成一种立体结构(三维)。当我们将一张纸变成一架纸飞机时，我们就采用了折叠的手法，赋予纸张以新功能。然而通过折叠，还会产生很多新的产品，相关的例子不胜枚举。在本书这两个练习中，我们调查研究这些可能性与操作方法，这是一种很有意义的研究模式，可以通过适度的手段来达成。

“覆层”是实际设计过程的另一领域，即将某种材料的镀层附在产品表面，以使其永久保持外形。通常情况下，产品表面永久性的覆层都是用持久耐用的材料制作，如金属板、塑料等。用这种方式制成的覆层可以为解决长期以来的设计难题—形状与内涵，提供新的解决视角。

## The Packaging Exercise

A simple exercise to train inventiveness and understanding of material can be done by packaging a simple product with paper or cardboard. If you understand the characteristics of paper, you have already learned a lot Packagíng products is a specialist job, which usually is done by specialised designers, but isopen for everybody who has goodideas and knows how to handle the material. Packages have become a prominent selling point. The package must first of all fulfil its function to protect and support a product. But in addition it conveys a meaning. It can tell the consumer what is inside, arouse his interest, display lifestyle, convey intelligence or wit, simplicity, tradition, abundance, or ecological consideration. A package is thegarment of a product and, just as human clothing, aims to clad the product and support its personality.

Design a package for ping pong balls with paper or cardboard using only a cutteras tool.

## Approaches:

First test the material, its pliability, its structure, its texture and colour. Try packaging eggs, ping-pong balls, soap, cakes, or other simple things to start with. Check its practical functions; protection, handling, transport, stacking, or recycling. Check it for its meaning and expression, judge its formal and semantic quality and develop criteria for its evaluation.

## ·包装练习

通过使用纸张或纸板包装一种简单产品的练习，可以训练一个人的创新性，以及对材料特征的理解。如果你理解了纸张的特征，那么你已经知道很多。包装产品是一种非常特殊的工作，通常情况下是由经过特殊训练的设计师操作的，但实际上，这项工作也可以由富有绝佳创意的、了解如何处理材料的任何人来完成。包装已经成为一种众所周知的产品卖点之一。首先，包装必须起到最基本的保护和宣传产品的作用，而另外，它还蕴含着其他意义。它还可以告知消费者产品的信息，引起他们的兴趣，展示一种生活方式，传递智慧或才智、简约、传统、丰盈或环保讯息。包装是产品的服装，就像人类的服装一样，旨在包裹产品，并展示产品自身的个性。

使用纸张或纸板设计一种乒乓球的包装，工具仅可使用切割刀。

方法：

首先检验材料的柔韧性、结构、质地和颜色。可以先试试包装鸡蛋、乒乓球、肥皂、蛋糕或其他简单的东西。检查其实用效果如何：保护性、手提便携性、运输、储藏或回收等是否方便等。检查它是否传达了产品的信息和所要表达的含义，评判它的形式和语义质量，并总结出相关的评估标准。

## The Folding Exercise

Folding is a very elementary process, turning a flat piece of paper, cardboard, a sheet of metal, or even plywood, if you apply hinges to it, into a threedimensional object. It is related to construction, for it creates a stable structure, a space saving object, an artistic expression, an ornamental object, or a sculptural effect.

Design a stable construction from a flat material, cut and joined by hinges, that first forms a flat package, but when unfolded you could either sit or lie on or use as a table or shelf.

The folding exercise has many authors. I was inspired by Werner Schmidt, an architect in Switzerland, who made it part of a workshop at the State Academy in Stuttgart and whose results were shown at the Furniture Fair in Milan.

I have adapted the idea to basic resources for my workshops in China. Its objective is to experience construction, function, human factors, and aesthetics. It should provide the opportunity to ask a great number of questions and to discover new criteria.

## Approaches:

Cardboard or foam-core sheets joined by adhesive tape are a good start. Concentrate on their structural and aesthetic qualities. Encourage new technologies and applications. Open the mind for new discoveries.

## ·折叠练习

折叠是一种非常基本的操作过程，即将平整的纸张、纸板、一张金属片甚至胶合板(需安装折页)变成一种三维立体结构。它与构成息息相关，因为它创造了一种稳固的结构、节省空间的形体、艺术化的表现手法、具有装饰性的物件或者是具有雕塑感的效果。

使用平整的材料设计一种稳固的结构，切割，并用折页连接，这样首先就做成了一个平整的包装。而当拆开它时，你可以坐在上面、躺在上面，或把它当作桌子或架子使用。

关于折叠的练习，我的灵感来自许多人。比如华纳·施密特，一位瑞士建筑师。他把这项练习作为斯图加特国立学院工作坊实践的一部分，而后，实践的成果在米兰举办的家具展会上展出。

我已经在我的中国工作坊的基础资源库中适当采用了这项练习的做法，目的是让学生们体验构成、功能、有关于人的因素以及美学因素的作用。它会给学生们提出大量问题，以及发现新标准的机会。

方法：

由胶带连接的纸板或者泡沫板即可。集中注意力观察它们的结构与美学质量，并鼓励应用新的技术应用成果。广开思路，探索新发现。

## The Cladding Exercise

Cladding components, whether they are mechanical or electronic, is an essential part of a designer's everyday job. It involves finding the form for the skin around components and turning it into astructure with a meaningful shape.

Think of a car with an engine, passenger compartment, and luggage space. Thinkof a hair dryer, with heating unit, fan, motor, and handle, or a coffee machine with water tank, heating unit, coffee filter and container for the coffee. After its structure has been settled, the distributed volumes need a skin or housing. The components must be tied together and begiven a logical, pleasing and convincing shape. The cladding exercise aims to stimulate different solutions to a standard design problem.

Design a skin or housing around two given volumes.

## Approaches:

Havea look atyour environment; there are innumerable examples that you might view in a new way. There are basically two different ways: the simple box in which you can put anything-the tectonic approach or, like the human body with the skin closely following theskeleton and muscles-the biological approach. There is no one single way. You might ask yourself whether to follow the given shape closely or to be generous without being afraid of packaging some air. You might consider whether youwant to show what is underthe skin and wonder what could be a meaningful shape. Youmight think ofdifferentways of generating a shape and try them.Expand your design skills by testing a variety of approaches andso develop your professional repertoire.

" It's a way of abstract thinking and opens a new view at the products around us."(Tank Mingming, First YearStudent)

## ·覆层练习

为物体覆层，不管是使用机械方式还是电子方式，都是设计师每日工作中必不可少的组成部分。它包括找出可以包裹物体四周的镀层的形式，然后再把它变成一种合适而富有意义的结构。

设想一下，一辆汽车由引擎部分、客舱部分和后备箱部分构成。再想一下，一个吹风机由加热装置、风扇、发动机和手柄组成，或者一部咖啡机，由水槽、加热装置、咖啡过滤器和盛放咖啡的容器构成。当覆层的具体结构确定之后，那么这个物体就需要一层外膜或外罩。该物体的组成部分必须牢固地连接在一起，并保持一种合乎逻辑的、令人愉悦和信服的形状。覆层练习旨在为标准的设计问题寻找不同的解决方案。

为两个物体设计一层外膜或外罩。

方法：

观察你周围的环境，一定存在你有意愿以新的方法去审视的数不胜数的例子。一般来说有两种不同的方法：一种是简单的方法，如同盒子一样，你可以放任何东西进去无论是构造方式，或是生物方式都可以(就像人体的皮肤，与骨骼和肌肉是紧密相连的)。事实上并不存在一种简单的方法。你可以问自己，是否应该忠实地遵循物体本来的形状来设计包装，或者大方一些，不在意包进去一些空气。你或许会思考：是否应该展示“皮肤”下面的东西?什么形状才是富有意义的?你可能会想到制作“皮肤”外形的各种不同的方法，并去尝试。你可以通过尝试各种不同的方法来拓展自己的设计技能，从而扩展自己的专业知识储备。

“这是一种抽象思维的方式，它让我们从全新的视角观察周围的物体。”(唐明明，一年级学生)
