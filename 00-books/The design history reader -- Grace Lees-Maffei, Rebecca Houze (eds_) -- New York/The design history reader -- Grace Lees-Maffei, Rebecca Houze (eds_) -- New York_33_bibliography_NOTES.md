1. The Ministry of Supply issued the Control of Timber (No. 1) Order on 5 September 1939, two days after war was declared. This Order established che Ministry of Control of Timber. The Utility Furniture Scheme was controlled by many different laws issued by the government. These laws provide the historian with a precise historical chronology of the scheme. However, due to the continual changes in the supplies of raw materials, many of the laws enacted were revoked or re-enacted with amendments within weeks of being issued. The large number of different laws which were issued to control the production, marking, supply and price of furniture make the scheme's history a complex one.
