# CHRONOLOGY MATTHEW S. WITKOVSKY

This chronology follows the trajectory of Dada from 1911 until 1926, in Zurich, Berlin, Hannover, Cologne, New York, and Paris. Events within each city appear within color-coded horizontal bands. Small arrowheads, following the same color code, link pictures to specific cities. A seventh timeline traces Dada's development outside these six city centers, as well as artistic, social, and political occurrences of direct relevance to the movement. Quotations are taken almost entirely from period sources.The chronology is not exhaustive, but instead recounts key events in narrative form and aims to suggest Dada's rhetorical tenor and conceptual preoccupations.
