All of these designs are like the islands that portend the existence of an undiscovered continent. Let us imagine through these prophetic islands a more stupendous continent of design. I believe that technology will bloom when planted on this sort of sensory perception. The HAPTIC Exhibition delivers this message. Today information spreads all around the world before we even notice. If I only whisper “haptic” from the tip of East Asia might the people in London understand its meaning or those in Barcelona catch my murmur, and “haptic" be communicated. I think I'll whisper it, though my intent is hardly ambiguous. As long as the message is pure, the more gently it is spoken, the more forcefully it will reverberate into the world's receptive places, as a penetrating whisper.
