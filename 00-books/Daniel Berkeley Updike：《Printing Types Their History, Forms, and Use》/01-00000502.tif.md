# STANDING RULES OF # The Stereotype Office. 1. Nothing is to be printed against Religion. 2. Every thing is to be avoided, upon the subject of Politics, which is offensive to any Party. 3. The Characters of Individuals are not to be attacked. 4. Every Work which is stereotyped at this Office, is to be composed with beautiful Types. 5. All the Stereotype Plates are to be made according to the improved Process discovered by EARL STANHOPE. 6. School Books, and all Works for the Instruction of Youth, will be stereotyped at a lower Price than any other. 330. Types used by the Stereotype Office, London, 1804
