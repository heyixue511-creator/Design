## Rietveld and his radical approach to mass production

Gerrit Thomas Rietveld(1888- 1964) was a Dutchcabinetmaker and later became internationally known as an architect. In 1919 he joined Holland's “De Stijl” movement, whose intention was to create an objective and universal language in art and design by omitting all personal manifestations. Its spiritual head was Theovan Doesburg(1883– 1931), who developed a theory called Neoplasticism by decomposing volumes into horizontal and vertical planes and reducing visual language to the primary colours of red, blue, yellow, and black, white, and grey. By doing this he wanted to keep the means of artistic expression pure and so create a new aesthetic. Many European artists belonged to the movement, of whom the best known is PietMondrian( 1872 – 1944).

When Rietveld built his“red-blue-chair”, which was only a prototype, he surely did not know that it would become an icon of modern design history. It showed the principles of Neoplasticism, using puregeometric elements and colour coding, red for vertical planes, blue for horizontal planes, and yellow for the exosed cut ends of the struts, and so related it to a virtual neoplastic space. Its elementary construction shows a radical approach to manufacture and mass production. Rietveld's constructions stimulated the Bauhaus designers and generations to follow.

## ·里特维尔德和他对于大众制造的激进手法

格利特·托马斯·里特维尔德(1888—1964)曾是一位荷兰家具木工，后来成为了国际知名的建筑师。1919年他加入了荷兰的“风格派”运动，该运动的目的是在艺术和设计领域，通过忽略所有个人主观的表征形式，创造出一种客观而普遍适用的语言形式。该活动的精神领袖是里奥·凡·杜斯堡(1883—1931)。他提出了一种被称作“新造型主义”的理论体系，该理论把立体分解成多个水平和垂直的平面，将视觉语言的表现形式简化为红、蓝、黄、黑、白、灰等基础色彩。通过这种做法，他试图将艺术表现的形式变得纯粹化，这样一来就诞生了一种全新的美学概念。许多欧洲艺术家都支持这次运动，其中属彼埃·蒙德里安(1872—1944)最为出名。

当里特维尔德构建自己的“红蓝椅子”，其仅仅是一个模型之时，他当然不会知道它会成为现代设计历史中的标志性符号。它完美地诠释了新造型主义的基本原则，使用纯粹的几何元素和色彩编码：红色代表垂直平面，蓝色代表水平平面，黄色代表裸露在外的支架杆切割末端，并将以上元素合并，建立起一个虚拟的新造型主义空间。其基础构成形式展示了一种可用于大众制造的激进手法。里特维尔德的构成方法得到了包豪斯设计师以及其后的追随者的认同，并赋予他们灵感。
