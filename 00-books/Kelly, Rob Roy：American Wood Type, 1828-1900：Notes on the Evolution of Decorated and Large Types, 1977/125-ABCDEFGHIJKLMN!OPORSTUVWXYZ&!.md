# ABCDE FGHIJ KLMN! OPORS TUVW XYZ&! Antique Tuscan Extended. First shown by Wells & Webb in their 1854 specimens. This cutting by the Hamilton Company. Lowercase and figures are missing. W hen this font of type was found, the capital "W" still had glued to its side a paper wrapper indicating the type was manufactured in 1881, the frst year Hamilton was in business. This specimen is printed from a veneer type.
