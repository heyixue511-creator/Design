# 90s CERAMICS & GLASS than in the past. Cross-overs with other disciplines have led Hadrian Pigott to achieve success within the art world, and enabled Dai Rees to diversify into fashion. The history of ceramics is appropriated in the figurative work of Martin Moore, which caricatures the conventions of Staffordshire figures. Significant advances have been made in glass practice, considering that this is only the third decade in the history of the studio glass movement' (although this movement has never been cohesive and encompasses many individual and diverse styles and techniques). Reflecting these advances, the 1992 College prospectus states: Upon graduation, a student may become a professional maker, a designer for the glass or ceramic industry, a decorator of industrial ware or a creator of architectural decoration.
