Of Rand's work, Moholy-Nagy wrote in 194o that he was impressed by its refreshing optimism in these confusing days’.8 Rand became the inspiration for many young designers of the 195os and 196os. In Rand's classes at Yale, the doctrines of Modernism offered structure, discipline and a certain fundamental reasoning.
