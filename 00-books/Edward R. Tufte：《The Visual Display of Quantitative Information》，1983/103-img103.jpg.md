## The effect extends beyond the ink of the design to the whole page. When exploited by the experts, such as Bridget Riley and Victor Vasarely, op art effects are undoubtedly eye-catching. But statistical graphics are also often drawn up so as to shimmer. This moiré vibration, probably the most common form of graphical clutter, is inevitably bad art and bad data graphics.
