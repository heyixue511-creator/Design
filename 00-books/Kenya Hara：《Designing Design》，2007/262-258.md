The guiding principle of the housing style that MUJI proposes is "editing."A living space isn't designed for the sake of convenient construction. The natural conception of an ideal style of housing is nurtured over time, simply through living. Some want to enjoy a vivacious, dynamic life, with a beautiful kitchen at the center of the home, where they can entertain guests. Others want the joy of sitting at a grand piano and tickling the ivories at any time of the day or night. Others want a large bath to which to retreat in rest and relaxation. Other people dream of spending their days reading, in rooms whose walls are lined with custom-made bookshelves. One of our ideals is to allow each and every one of these people to independently “edit" his housing environment in search of the one most compatible with his personal lifestyle.
