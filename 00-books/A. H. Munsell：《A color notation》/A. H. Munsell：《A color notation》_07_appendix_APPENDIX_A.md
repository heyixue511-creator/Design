## APPENDIX A

Contrast is another subtle element of coloristic art. The simplest form appears when two colors in contact seem different from what they would when viewed separately. Their interval seems to be increased because the lighter of the two appears more luminous, while the darker appears still more dark than it is. Each becomes tinged with the complement of the other, which may enhance or degrade it, according to what colors are chosen.

Consciously or not, all skillful use of color has to reckon with these very important factors.
