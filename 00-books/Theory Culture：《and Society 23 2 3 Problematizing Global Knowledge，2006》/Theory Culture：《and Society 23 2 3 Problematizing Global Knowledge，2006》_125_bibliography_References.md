Bracha L. Ettinger is an artist, clinical psychologist, psychoanalyst and theorist in the fields of female sexuality, Lacanian and inter-subjective psychoanalysis, and aesthetics. Her paintings have been exhibited in major international museums of contemporary art. She is the author of The Matrixial Borderspace (Minnesota University Press, 2006).
