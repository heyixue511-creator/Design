When producing an exhibition, I usually don't create products for it, but this time I broke my own rules by making this “water pachinko"game. (Pachinko is a Japanese form of pinball.) For this exhibition, we did some preliminary research into various technologies that would be appropriate and available to the participants' designs. At the time, I was so interested in the technology and phenomenon of super-hydrophobic surfaces that I couldn't suppress my creative appetite.
