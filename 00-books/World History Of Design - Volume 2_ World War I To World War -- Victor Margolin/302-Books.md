Australia; Elizabeth Butel, Margaret Preston: The Art of Constant Rearrangement, Heather Johnson, Roy De Maistre: The Australian Years, 1894–1930; and Anne McDonald, Douglas Annand: The Art of Life

Information on industrialization in New Zealand is scattered throughout various sources. On the crafts and domestic arts several books were helpful: Ann Calhoun, The Arts and Crafts Movement in New Zealand, 1870–1940: Women Make Their Mark, and Douglas Lloyd Jenkins, At Home: A Century of New Zealand Design. Christopher Thompson's MA thesis, Confronting Design: Case Studies in the Design of Ceramics in New Zealand, provided extensive information on interwar ceramics. On graphic design, Hamish Thompson's Paste Up: A Century of New Zealand Poster Art and Richard Wolfe's Well Made New Zealand: A Century of Trademarks were the basic sources.
