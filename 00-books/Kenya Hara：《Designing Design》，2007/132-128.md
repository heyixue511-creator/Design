![](images/a17fea99c83551971df1c8b84e6190d52480e72829448c3ece44eeb154d23d29.jpg) ## Masayo Ave: 800 dots—Paperback Cover Masayo Ave, the product designer working from Milan for no less than 16 years, moved her base to Berlin in 2006, where she also puts great effort into education. She proposed a paperback cover. The paper is printed with foaming ink used for Braille. The pattern is a traditional Japanese one called same-komon (sharkskin dots). Book jackets usually feature patterns and logotypes, putting us in mindof graphic design, but Ave's design stands on simple tactility alone.
