The fact that the clinic constantly maintains the cleanliness of this signage illustrates its dedication to utmost hygiene for the sake of both patients and visitors. This provides increased peace of mind to those spending timehere before andafter childbirth. This design solution is based on the same principle as the use of white tablecloths by first-class restaurants. If these establishments wanted to conceal the unavoidable soiling of a restaurant tabletop, they would use darkcolored or vinyl tablecloths. But high-caliber restaurants dare to use pure white cloth in part to make obvious to the customer that their service includes first-ratecleanliness. Signage is intrinsically a mere “indication,"functioning as a guidance tool. And as long as it exists in space, signage cannot escape from being some kind of physical object. Letters, characters and symbols cannot stay afloat in mere air, sosignage is generally displayed on a substance like acrylic, metal, wood or glass. This is one aspect of the destiny of signage design.
