MUJI's perspective is unique; in the MUJI concept, design intervenes in the makingof things. This counters the rest of the world, which runs on the fuel of capital and appetite. Japan, looking upon the world from its detached location at the eastern end of Asia, has built anaesthetic that is infinitely attractive to human rationality, not within luxury and extravagance, but simplicity. Doesn't affluence lie within understatement? This is what MUJI asks the world.
