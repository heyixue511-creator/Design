·A175-A177 # Designs for Rapid-Transit Line from Gesundbrunnen to Neukölln 1911-12 The AEG railway line from Gesundbrunnen to Neukölln was planned principally as an underground railway, with short elevated sections. For the elevated sections, Behrens designed steel viaducts resting on single-pillar supports (illustration Al76) and an overhead station with a covered central platform and entrance staircase. The station design has survived in photographs of two slightly varying drawings. The plan to build the railway was abandoned after the war on economic grounds. ## Literature Wittig 1922, p. 9; Buddensieg and Rogge 1976, p. 102; Sabine Bohle-Heintzenberg, Architektur der Berliner Hoch- und Untergrundbahn (Berlin, 1980).
