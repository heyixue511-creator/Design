![](images/667d54e9176a93bb3194002fb37419c0dba158a16d4e3105328a25f80bbdff68.jpg) ## Shunji Yamanaka: FloatingCompass The product designer Shunji Yamanaka devised a floating compass with the structure of a water strider. Yamanaka is a robotics expert. Through designs rangingfrom robots to smart cards at automatic wickets, he has longworked on the integration of technology and human beings. His design solution for this project is a small compass shaped like a water strider. Ultra-hydrophobic coating technology makes the surface of the normally absorbent paper highly water repellent.
