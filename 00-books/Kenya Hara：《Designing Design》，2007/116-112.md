In the center of this small and precisely cut paper creature is a small stick of magnetic iron. For the exhibition, the compasses were floated on water in a very thin acrylic basin about 2 mm deep. Controlled by a magnet under the basin, the compasses rotated in harmony, fluent and smooth. This extraordinary accord is really haptic, in that the senses are also evoked by especially smooth sensations, not only those that are hairy or bristled.
