# Cambridge Middle East Library Editorial Board ROGER OWEN (Chair) SHAUL BAKHASH MICHAEL C. HUDSON NOAH LUCAS DENIZ KANDIYOTI RASHID KHALIDI BASIM MUSSALLAM SHIMON SHAMIR MALCOLM YAPP The Cambridge Middle East Library aims to bring together outstanding scholarly work on the history, politics, sociology and economics of the Middle East and North Africa in the nineteenth and twentieth centuries. While primarily focussing on monographs based on original research, the series will also incorporate broader surveys and in-depth treatments. A list of books in this series will be found at the end of this volume
