![](images/a28305bafd64e99d6b2a0d2584140db8f7ce0d2b5d54a8a46eaa8d79cbee9078.jpg) # EXFORMATION A New Information Format Graduation projects at Musashino Art University, Department of Science of Design Making the World Unknown What constantly invigorates the human mind is the unknown; we aren't animated by what we already know, but we're eager to make the world known. In today's context, in which media continues to evolve, every event and phenomenon processed into information circulates with high density at high speed. By constantly keeping in touch with those pieces of information, people continue to replace the unknown world with identified events and phenomena. To know something is to impregnate the senses with an inspirational, vital, exciting experience.
